🧮 Risk Calculator

Real traders never ask "how much can I make?" first. They ask "how much could I lose?" This calculator uses your bot's exact formula: risk only 1% of your money on any single trade.

Fill in the boxes and press Calculate!

Why only 1%? 🤔

If you risk 1% per trade, you could be wrong 20 times in a row and still have over 80% of your money left. If you risk 25% per trade, just 4 bad trades and you're almost broke. Small risks = you get to keep playing. Survival is the superpower.

🛡️ The Golden Rules

These come straight from your bot's research. Print them. Tape them to the wall. 😄

1️⃣
Never risk more than 1% on one trade. Small risks mean you survive to trade another day.
🛑
Always set your stop-loss BEFORE you buy. Decide where you're wrong ahead of time (the bot uses −3%).
🎲
A good-looking backtest is NOT proof. Your bot's edge test showed +20% returns can still be no better than random (z-score 0.29). Demand z > 2 with 100+ trades.
🏆
Don't chase win rate. Your bot wins only 27% of the time but still makes money — because wins ($4.12 avg) are 4× bigger than losses ($1.07 avg). Expectancy beats win rate.
🔧
Don't keep tuning until the past looks perfect. That's overfitting — the #1 way traders lose real money.
🏛️
"Secret" signals usually aren't. Politician trades looked exciting, but the edge disappears before the 45-day disclosure deadline. Test everything.
📄
Paper trade for weeks before using real money. If practice doesn't work, real money won't either.
🐢
Slow is smooth, smooth is fast. Most traders lose by rushing. Being patient IS the edge.
